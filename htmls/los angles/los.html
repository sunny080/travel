About Los Angeles

Los Angeles (US: /lɔːs ˈændʒələs/ (About this soundlisten) lawss AN-jəl-əs;[a] Tongva: Tovaangar;[13][14] Spanish: Los Ángeles; "The Angels"), often spoken and written as its initialism, L.A., is the largest city in California. With a 2020 population of 3,898,747 ,[15] it is the second-largest city in the United States, after New York City
